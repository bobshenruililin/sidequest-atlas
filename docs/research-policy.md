# Research policy

Never fabricate events, prices, schedules, or opening hours.

Source priority:

1. Official venue/company pages
2. Government and transit authorities
3. Universities
4. Annual reports / IR (label the period)
5. National statistical agencies
6. Central banks, OECD, academic sources
7. Reputable local publications
8. Community sources for subjective texture only

Every volatile fact should be a `Claim` with `sourceIds`, verification metadata, and `reverifyAfter`.

For Nordic Fieldwork, August means **August 2026**.
