## English opening (~75 seconds; soft identity)

Good afternoon. My name is Shen Ruililin — Bob. I study Global Health and Development at HKU, and the question I keep returning to is how public health can reach ordinary lives through systems people actually trust.

My background is a mix of research, fieldwork, service, and teaching. My GPA is 4.24 out of 4.30. Through Laidlaw, I study heat and cardiovascular admissions, not as an abstract academic topic, but as preparation for better hospital readiness and public-health response. Through Wu Zhi Qiao, Food Angel, Refugee Union, peer teaching, and STEM TA work, I have learned that service begins with listening before offering solutions.

Long term, I hope to work at the seam between Hong Kong and the Mainland in cross-boundary disease control. Hong Kong has legal, professional, and international trust; the Mainland has scale and urgent public-health needs. I want to help make that cooperation more trusted and usable.

I am applying because this Foundation’s mission matches the person I am trying to become: rooted in Hong Kong, serious about understanding China, and committed to public service.

所以我嘅志向，係將研究變成服務嘅前提。長遠我希望做香港同內地之間，公共衞生合作嗰條「縫」上面嘅人，特別係跨境疾控同醫院準備。

我申請呢個獎學金，唔係想將履歷表再加長。我係希望有一個群體，逼我更認真咁理解中國、服務香港，亦用自己嘅訓練幫到更多人。多謝。
